Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RunBeta

Can I schedule a service for another day or time?
Absolutely! You can schedule services for any date and time that works for you, weeks or even months in advance. The app shows real-time availability of professionals, and you can choose time slots that fit your schedule.
Does Runbeta offer a customer support if I have a problem?
Yes, we have 24/7 support. You can reach us through the in-app chat, email, or our dedicated support line for any assistance you need.
What if I am not satisfied with what I received?
Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're unhappy with a service, we'll investigate and either have it rectified or offer a refund according to our service guarantee.
What happens if the professional I book doesn't show up?
If a professional doesn't show up, we'll quickly assign a replacement or provide a full refund of your payment.
How much does it cost to use Runbeta?
Downloading and browsing the app is free. We only charge for the services you book, with transparent upfront pricing.
Can I set my own prices?
Yes, service providers have full control over their pricing and service details.
Is there a provider community?
Yes, we foster a community where providers can network, share tips, and grow together through exclusive events and forums.
How do I grow my visibility?
Consistent high ratings and completed jobs boost your profile's ranking, making you more visible to potential customers.
Can I handle multiple service category?
Yes! You can list and verify skills across multiple categories to expand your earning potential.
What if I get a low rating?
We provide feedback and resources to help you improve. Repeated low ratings may lead to account review to maintain our quality standards.
Can I integrate Runbeta with my existing systems?
Yes, RunBeta offers robust API integrations to connect with your existing CRM or operational software.
What can of analysis does Runbeta provide?
We provide AI-powered insights into team performance, customer demand, and revenue trends to help you optimize your business.
Is there a fee for companies to join?
We offer flexible partnership models. Contact our enterprise team for a plan tailored to your company's scale.
Can I handle multiple staff members?
Yes, our dashboard is built to manage large teams with centralized dispatching and tracking.
How can Runbeta help my Service company grow?
RunBeta provides centralized team management, AI-powered analytics, and a smart dispatch system to optimize your operations and increase efficiency.
